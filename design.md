## name: design
description: A design framework for creating "Brand-Immersive Conversion" interfaces, blending high-trust minimalist usability with high-energy editorial brand storytelling.

### Design Objective

Synthesize the clarity of modern fintech (Wealthsimple) with the emotional impact of global consumer brands (Coca-Cola) and the lifestyle aesthetics of boutique fashion/e-commerce (Garoa, Havaianas). The goal is to move users through a high-trust conversion funnel without sacrificing brand personality.

### Core Principles

#### 1. Emotional Minimalism

* **The Look:** Utilize aggressive whitespace and "fintech clean" layouts to reduce cognitive load.
* **The Twist:** Inject a single, high-saturation brand color (e.g., Coca-Cola Red) into the interface's key focal points (primary CTAs, notification badges, or hero accents) to keep the site feeling "alive" rather than clinical.
* **Implementation:** Use white or very light gray backgrounds with high-contrast, dark-mode-ready typography.

#### 2. Editorial Product Integration

* **The Look:** Reject the standard e-commerce grid. Use asymmetric layouts that treat the web page like a physical magazine spread.
* **The Twist:** Seamlessly blend high-quality lifestyle photography (people interacting with products) directly alongside utilitarian UI elements.
* **Implementation:** Use full-bleed imagery that overlaps with text boxes or "Shop the Look" buttons. Ensure that the "buying" action feels like a natural progression of the "story."

#### 3. High-Trust Typographic Hierarchy

* **The Look:** Use two distinct font families.
* **Headings:** Use a bold, expressive, or custom brand-heavy font for H1s and H2s to establish personality.
* **Functional Text:** Use a highly legible, utilitarian sans-serif for body copy, pricing, labels, and navigation.
* **Implementation:** Ensure a minimum 1.5 line-height for body text and use uppercase for small labels to create a sense of order.

#### 4. Tactile Micro-Interactions

* **The Look:** The interface should respond physically to user input.
* **Implementation:**
* **Buttons:** Subtle scale-down on click and smooth color shifts on hover.
* **Scrolling:** Use lazy-loading "fade-in" animations for images as they enter the viewport.
* **States:** Design clear active/hover/pressed states that provide immediate visual feedback, making the UI feel premium and responsive.



### Application Workflow

1. **Map the Journey:** Identify where the user needs "Trust" (Wealthsimple style) vs. where they need "Excitement" (Havaianas style).
2. **Visual Balance:** Apply the 60-30-10 rule: 60% whitespace/neutrals, 30% photography/editorial content, 10% high-energy brand color.
3. **Prototyping:** Use auto-layout for rigid precision in data-heavy sections, but break the grid in hero and storytelling sections to maintain an editorial feel.
